What is NSTP EMS?

A comprehensive digital solution for managing student enrollment in the National Service Training Program (NSTP). It streamlines registration, eliminates manual processes, and provides real-time analytics.

Key Benefits:

  • Automated enrollment process with validation
  • Real-time tracking of student participation
  • Secure role-based access (Admin & Students)
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics
  • 24/7 availability for students and staff
